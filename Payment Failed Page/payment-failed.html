Common Payment Issues

Why was my payment declined?

Payments can be declined for several reasons: insufficient funds, daily transaction limits, security blocks by your bank, expired payment method, or technical errors. Check your account balance and contact your bank if unsure.

Was money deducted from my account?

No money is deducted for failed transactions. If you see a temporary hold on your account, it will be released within 1-3 business days. This is an authorization hold that banks place during payment attempts.

How can I avoid payment failures?

1. Ensure sufficient balance in your account
2. Check your daily transaction limits
3. Use a stable internet connection
4. Update your payment app
5. Contact your bank to authorize online payments

What should I do if money was deducted but payment failed?

Contact our support team immediately with your transaction details. We'll work with the payment processor to either complete the transaction or initiate a refund. Refunds typically take 3-7 business days.

Can I save my payment method for future use?

Yes, you can save your payment method securely in your account settings. We use tokenization to store payment references without storing actual card details. This makes future payments faster and more secure.

Is there a transaction fee for payments?

No, ZewedJobs does not charge any additional transaction fees. You only pay the amount shown on your invoice. Your bank or payment provider may charge their standard fees, which we have no control over.
