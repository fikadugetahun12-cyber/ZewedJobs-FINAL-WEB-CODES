Payment FAQs

Is my payment secure?

Yes, all payments are processed using 256-bit SSL encryption and comply with PCI DSS security standards. We never store your full card details on our servers.

How long does payment processing take?

Mobile money payments are processed instantly. Bank transfers take 1-2 hours during business hours. Card payments are immediate.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes, we automatically email a payment receipt to the email address you provide. You can also download it from your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major Ethiopian payment methods including Telebirr, HelloCash, Amole, CBE Birr, and bank transfers. We also accept international Visa and Mastercard payments.

Is VAT included in the price?

Yes, all prices include 15% VAT as required by Ethiopian law. The VAT breakdown is shown clearly in your invoice.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all our services. Contact our support team within 7 days of payment for refund requests.
